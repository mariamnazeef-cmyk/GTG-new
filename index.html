Our Mission Statement

Envision a world in which every girl is given an equal right to education - regardless of her socioeconomic status, ethnicity, or where she lives. Give to Girls is a non-profit organization that aims to make this vision a reality. By providing young women with the proper resources and opportunities to build a better future, Give to Girls mobilizes educational support, supplies, and funds to help girls who are missing from classrooms across the globe.
