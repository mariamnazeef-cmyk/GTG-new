Virtual English Lessons

Our Pilot Program

We believe education begins with confidence and the courage to speak. GiveToGirls is launching virtual English lessons to help girls build confidence, communication skills, and access to more educational opportunities.

01

Virtual Lessons

Students meet online with university student volunteers from the United States for guided English practice from anywhere in the world.

02

Conversation Practice

Each lesson focuses on everyday speaking, reading, vocabulary, and pronunciation in a supportive and interactive environment.

03

Confidence Building

The program gives girls a safe space to ask questions, make mistakes, and build confidence in their voice over time.
